Golang Developer

Apply Now!

We are looking for some Golang developer responsible for the development, maintenance, and deployment of applications. Your primary focus will be developing enterprise applications for customers following proper architecture patterns and clean code. Therefore, a commitment to collaborative problem solving, sophisticated design, and creating quality products is essential.

Skills

  • Minimum of 2 years working experience in GoLang
  • Minimum of 3 years working experience on Agile projects and strong knowledge of Agile terminology and tools like Asana/JIRA.
  • Must have vast experience with Go test code.
  • Experience in the development of a Billing System including integration with payment gateways such as Stripe, PayPal, or any other recognized payment gateway will be a plus.
  • Database design experience with PostgreSQL.
  • Deep knowledge and clear understanding of design patterns, object-oriented concepts, and implementation.
  • Knowledge of Python and Python frameworks will be a plus.
  • Experience with web service APIs (REST, SOAP, gRPC).
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills are a must.

Responsibilities

  • Looking for Golang developer with experience implementing Gin Framework-based enterprise applications.>
  • Support technical systems development team with design, development, implementation, testing, and delivery of solutions for a current international client
  • Ability to develop technical solutions, and document the solution architecture and design using standard design tools

Educational Requirements

  • B.Sc./M.Sc in Computer Science or Software Engineering from any reputed university/Equivalent combination of education and experience may be considered.
