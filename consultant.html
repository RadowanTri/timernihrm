Time research & innovation

Time research & innovation is a research organisation based in the United Kingdom and Bangladesh. We have started various research in behavioural computer sciences; technology, health informatics, education and community. Our supreme emphasis is to execute research, training and development activities initially in the UK and Bangladesh to help disadvantaged people. Our Chair and CEO Shah Siddiqui is currently working on ‘ANGTHAS’ an Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning project as his PhD research at the University of Portsmouth, UK.
